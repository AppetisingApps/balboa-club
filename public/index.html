Balboa Club

Dublin, Ireland
Balboa is a type of swing dance. The footwork is more minimal than Lindy Hop and typically danced to fast music. We also practice a little of "Slow Bal", which might be described as romantic in spirit. Balboa Club is a new project in the Dublin swing dancing scene.

Weekly Practice
Important: do not attend unless you are signed up to the weekly newsletter. There are often last-minute changes of venue and you need to RSVP :)
What is practice like: The weekly practice is structured, guided, focused and we dance in a rotation as in class. It is not a free-form session.
Level: it is not aimed at beginners. However, you are most welcome to come if you are a beginner, are commited, and are okay with perhaps being a little out of your depth for a while!

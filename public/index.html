What to Expect
The weekly practice is structured, guided, focused and we dance in a rotation as in class. It is not a free-form session. The aim is to cover two new moves, and one/two short sequences involving previous moves each week.
Level: it is not aimed at beginners. However, you are most welcome to come if you are a beginner, are committed, and are okay with perhaps being a little out of your depth for a while!
What is Balboa?
Balboa is a type of swing dance. The footwork is more minimal than Lindy Hop and typically danced to fast music. We also practice a little of "Slow Bal", which might be described as romantic in spirit. Balboa Club is a new project in the Dublin swing dancing scene.
